Fentech  /  About Us

Professional export PVC fence

HANGZHOU FANTAI PLASTIC CO. LTD is located in Hangzhou, the beautiful tourist city of Zhejiang Province, professionally exports PVC fences to North America, Australia, Europe, Middle East, and South Africa, and more and more countries and regions interests in PVC/Vinyl fence. Fentech fences are manufactured with virgin PVC/Vinyl material and UV Protected. The material is lightweight and easy to install, with a DIY option for the handyman. PVC/Vinyl Fence has been more and more popular in recent years due to its durability and the lack of need for maintenance. More than that, lots of plus points you will gain by selecting PVC Fence: NO PAINT, only need general cleaning. Saves your time and money. NO FADING does not lose its color at all. NO ROT, no fear of Vinyl fences rusting or rotting or getting decomposed like other materials. NO WORRIES! Non-toxic as compared to some of the other fencing materials. The available colors are white, grey, and tan. We supply picket fence, pool fence, horse fence, privacy fence, semi-privacy fence and railings and matching gates for villa, garden, park, pool, ranch, etc. Post---Posts are the most important member of a fence section. It holds the rails & the section in place. Posts take the load of the fence and are either mounted on a flat surface with a flange or are driven 2 to 3 feet under the ground. The Posts are routed in such a way that they accommodate the rails. Line Post---This constitutes the largest part of most fence jobs. They are usually at every 8 feet or 6 feet section depending on the size of the sections used for fencing. Line posts are routed on both sides (opposite to each other) so as to accommodate rails/ sections from each side. Corner Post---These posts are used on corners & have their adjacent faces routed. So they give the desired corner angle in the fence. End Post---These are routed only on one side and are used in ends usually by wall or pillar. A routed hole on one side accommodates the section while the other end is not routed. End Gate Post---These are usually designed stronger than regular posts in most cases to hold the Gate. These posts are routed only on one side to receive the fence section. Blank Post / Blank Gate Post ---These Posts are never routed at all. Blank Posts are mostly used where you have the wall on one side & a gate on the other side of the post.
